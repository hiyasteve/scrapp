🐟 Scrapp

Scrapp is a community map of places where you can buy scraps ("pieces of deep-fried batter left over in the fryer as a by-product of frying, served as an accompaniment to chips"). They're called "bits" in yorkshire. the wikipedia page reckons they're called "gribbles" in the west country, but i'm not having that.

Please contribute to this important citizen science project by marking whether your local chippies (or the chippies you visit on your travels) sell scraps.

send me an email to stephen.b.carlton@gmail.com if you have any feedback or would like me to appear on your show on local radio.
